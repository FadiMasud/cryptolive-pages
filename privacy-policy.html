Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 September 2025

CryptoLive (“we”, “us”, or “the App”) provides cryptocurrency market information for informational purposes. We designed the App to minimize data collection and keep your information private.

1) Scope

This policy applies to the CryptoLive iOS/iPadOS app and our support pages under cryptolive.blog and GitHub Pages.

2) Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, identifiers, or precise location.
  • We do not request or store wallet private keys, seed phrases, or exchange login credentials.
  • We do not build advertising profiles or sell personal data.

Local-only storage: Your watchlist, price alerts, notes, and settings are stored on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.

3) Features & Data Handling (Current Version)

  • Local notifications only: Price alerts you create are scheduled and delivered locally on your device. No Apple Push Notification (APNs) is used in this version.
  • No subscriptions or in-app purchases: The current version does not include paid features.
  • No advertising or tracking: The current version does not display third-party ads and does not use ad tracking.
  • Diagnostics: We do not collect analytics. If you opt in to share diagnostics with developers in iOS settings, Apple may share aggregate crash/usage data per Apple’s policies.

4) Third-Party Services & Data Sources

To display prices and charts, the App uses:

  • CoinGecko API — public cryptocurrency market data requested from your device.
  • TradingView Chart Widget (via WebView) — the Pro Chart loads TradingView’s script (tv.js) from s3.tradingview.com inside an in-app web view to render charts. TradingView may use standard web technologies (e.g., cookies/local storage) under its own policies. We do not send them your personal data, but your device makes direct web requests to their servers to load chart assets.

Not in use: DEX Screener and other decentralized-token providers are not active in this version. If we add providers, we will update this section.

5) Data Retention

We do not maintain user accounts or server-side profiles. Preferences and alerts are stored on your device and are removed when you delete the App or reset the App’s data.

6) Your Choices

  • Turn notifications on/off in iOS Settings > Notifications > CryptoLive.
  • Delete the App to remove all locally stored data.

7) Children’s Privacy

CryptoLive is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

8) Security

We minimize data processing and use HTTPS for network requests. No system is 100% secure, but we work to keep the App current and protected.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page, with the “Last updated” date above.

10) Contact Us

Questions about privacy or this policy? Contact: support@cryptolive.blog
