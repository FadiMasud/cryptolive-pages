Quick start

  • Watchlist: Search a coin and add it to your list.
  • Price alerts: Open a coin, set a target price, then enable notifications.
  • Charts: Use the Pro Chart view for detailed timeframes and interaction.

Notifications

  • Push (APNs) + Local: Price alerts are delivered via Apple Push Notification service (APNs). Some alerts may also be scheduled locally on your device.
  • Ensure notifications are allowed in iOS Settings > Notifications > CryptoLive.
  • After updating, open the app once to register your device for push.

Data sources

  • CoinGecko API for cryptocurrency market data.
  • TradingView Chart Widget (WebView) for the Pro Chart.
  • Not in use: DEX Screener and other decentralized-token providers are not active in this version.

Troubleshooting

  • Check your internet connection.
  • Force-close and reopen the app.
  • Reinstall if issues persist (this resets local settings).
  • For alerts: confirm notifications are enabled and your alert threshold is sensible.

Known notes

  • No subscriptions and no third-party advertising in this version.
